Our Smart Farming Solutions

           

Discover how AgriPulse’s IoT technology transforms irrigation, livestock protection, and farm management for small-scale farmers.

                   
   
         
       
           
               
                   

Smart Automated Irrigation System (SAIS)

                    

                        SAIS revolutionizes irrigation with an ESP32-WROOM-32E microcontroller, precision soil moisture sensors, flow meters, and solar-powered LiPo batteries in an IP67 enclosure. Integrated with weather APIs, it automates 80% of irrigation tasks, saving 30–40% water and boosting yields by 10–20%.                    

                   
                           
  • AI-driven irrigation for maize, citrus, and more
    •                        
  • Leak detection and SMS/WhatsApp alerts
    •                        
  • Optional GSM module (R420 extra)
    •                        
  • Offline mode for remote farms
    •                    
                   

                        Pricing: R2,160 (Kit), R420 (Installation), R216/month (Basic), R336/month (Premium)

Livestock Tracker

                   

                        Our Livestock Tracker features discreet, tamper-proof GPS tags (5cm x 3cm, camouflaged) with solar-powered 6-month battery life. The ESP32-based base station supports real-time tracking and geofencing, reducing theft by 90% with silent alerts.                    

                   
                           
  • Stealth design mimics hide, no LEDs
    •                        
  • Tamper alerts via SMS/WhatsApp
    •                        
  • Herd analytics for health insights
    •                        
  • Solar-powered base station
    •                    
                   

                        Pricing: R1,800 (Base Station), R480/Tag, R96/month/Tag

AgriPulse App

                   

                        The AgriPulse App unifies SAIS and Livestock Tracker data in a mobile/web platform. Built with Python/Flask and MQTT, it offers real-time dashboards, customizable alerts, and crop/livestock analytics for smarter farm management.                    

                   
                           
  • Real-time soil, water, and GPS data
    •                        
  • Geofencing and movement alerts
    •                        
  • Crop health and herd behavior insights
    •
