Smart Automated Irrigation System (SAIS)

SAIS intelligently monitors your soil conditions in real-time and automatically waters your crops exactly when needed. By analyzing soil moisture levels and integrating with weather forecasts, it prevents both underwatering and overwatering, ensuring optimal growing conditions.

  • AI-driven irrigation customized for various crop types
  • Automatic leak detection with instant alerts
  • Cellular connectivity option for remote farms
  • Works offline in areas with poor connectivity

Pricing: R2,160 (Kit), R420 (Installation), R216/month (Basic), R336/month (Premium)

Livestock Tracker

Our Livestock Tracker provides 24/7 location monitoring for your valuable animals. The discreet, camouflaged tags are virtually undetectable to thieves yet provide you with continuous tracking. Create virtual boundaries and receive instant alerts if your livestock strays or is stolen.

  • Instant theft detection alerts
  • Movement pattern analysis for health monitoring
