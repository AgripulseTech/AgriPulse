About AgriPulse

Started by the AgriPulse team of South Africa, passionate innovators dedicated to transforming small-scale farming.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Empower small-scale farmers in KwaZulu-Natal, Eastern Cape, and Western Cape with affordable, intelligent IoT systems like SAIS and Livestock Tracker, including the AgriPulse App, for optimized irrigation and discreet livestock protection, driving productivity and sustainability.

Vision

Become South Africa’s premier provider of smart farming solutions, pulsing innovation into agriculture with autonomous technology and real-time insights for farmers through our integrated app and IoT systems.

Our Team

AgriPulse was started by a team of passionate innovators in South Africa, combining expertise in microcontroller programming, software development, hardware design, and farmer outreach to deliver cutting-edge IoT solutions.

Innovation Lead

Spearheads IoT development with expertise in JavaScript and Python.

Hardware Specialist

Farmer Outreach

Connects with farmers to ensure solutions meet real-world needs.
