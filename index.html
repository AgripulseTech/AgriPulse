Research & Impact

Tackling Water Scarcity

Western Cape faces R500M/year in drought-related losses, while KwaZulu-Natal loses R300M (Agri SA, 2023). Small-scale farmers spend 70–80% of their time on manual irrigation, with inefficient water use reducing yields. AgriPulse’s SAIS uses AI-driven irrigation, precision soil sensors, and weather integration to save 30–40% water and automate 80% of tasks. Unlike costly enterprise solutions (R20,000+), SAIS costs R2,160 with subscriptions from R216/month, offering unmatched affordability and crop-specific analytics for maize, citrus, and more.

Combating Livestock Theft

Livestock theft costs South Africa R1.4B annually, with Eastern Cape and KwaZulu-Natal as hotspots (SAPS, 2023). Traditional GPS tags (R500+) are bulky and visible, making them easy targets. AgriPulse’s Livestock Tracker uses discreet, tamper-proof tags (5cm x 3cm, camouflaged) with solar-powered 6-month battery life, reducing theft by 90%. Priced at R1,800 (base) and R480/tag, it offers silent alerts and geofencing, outpacing competitors with its stealth design and herd analytics.
