Frequently Asked Questions

           
               
                    

How does SAIS save water?

                   

                        SAIS uses AI-driven irrigation and precision soil sensors to optimize water use, saving 30–40% compared to manual methods. It integrates weather forecasts to avoid overwatering.                    

               
               
                   

Is the Livestock Tracker tamper-proof?

                   

                        Yes, tags use embedded screws and camouflaged designs. Removal triggers instant SMS/WhatsApp alerts, ensuring 90% theft reduction.                    

               
               
                   

Does the AgriPulse App work offline?

                   

                        Yes, the app supports offline mode for basic functions, syncing data when connectivity is restored, ideal for remote farms.                    

               
                
                   

What’s the warranty?

                   

                        All hardware (SAIS, Tracker) comes with a 1-year warranty. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for peace of mind.
